Computer Trivia

Game Format

  • Computer Trivia is a two player, 10 question trivia game.
  • Player 1 answers 10 multiple choice questions.
  • Each correct answer is worth 2 points.
  • Each incorrect answer is -1 point.
  • Player 2 then answers 10 multiple choice questions.
  • The player with the highest score wins.

    • Game Rules

  • No use of cell phones or other outside reference materials. Brain power only.
